Fundamental Analysis Process

1
Data Collection
Manual extraction from annual reports, quarterly results, NSE/BSE filings. Heavy Excel-based data entry (70% of time spent here).
2
Excel Model Update
Update 5-10 year historical data in sector-specific Excel models. Each analyst owns Excel sheets for their coverage.
3
Analysis & Forecasting
Financial modeling, peer comparison, growth analysis. Generate forecasts based on historical trends and market outlook.
4
Report Writing
Create 10-12 page detailed reports with charts, rationale, and recommendations (Buy/Hold/Sell with target prices).
5
Approval Workflow
Editor review ‚Üí HOD approval ‚Üí Compliance check (holdings, relationships, disclosure requirements).
6
Publishing
Manual data entry into MoEngage platform ‚Üí Distribution to clients via email, website, mobile app.

Technical Analysis Process

1
Market Data Analysis
Daily analysis of price/volume patterns using Bloomberg, Reuters. Focus on charts, indicators, and technical patterns.
2
Pattern Recognition
Identify technical patterns (triangles, support/resistance). Some automation available in Bloomberg for basic patterns.
3
Daily Report Creation
Generate daily market outlook by 8:30 AM. Short-term recommendations with entry/exit points and stop losses.
4
Intraday Updates
Real-time recommendations during market hours based on price movements and news flow.
5
Back Office Processing
Input recommendations into research system (EXE file) with ratings, targets, and rationale.
6
Distribution
Automated distribution through CMS to customer databases. Push notifications for urgent updates.
